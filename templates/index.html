How It Works

How MediScan AI Works

MediScan AI follows a structured AI-driven workflow to assist doctors in early disease detection using medical images.

1

Patient Data & Scan Upload

The clinician selects a detection module (Brain, Lung, Liver, Heart) and uploads the patient's CT, MRI, or X-ray scan along with basic details.

2

AI Model Analysis

A trained deep learning model processes the scan to identify abnormal patterns, lesions, or disease indicators.

3

Confidence Scoring

The system calculates a prediction confidence score that represents the likelihood of disease presence based on model inference.

4

Explainable AI Heatmap

A heatmap is generated to visually highlight regions of the scan that influenced the AI's decision, improving transparency and trust.

5

Clinical Interpretation

The AI output is presented as a decision-support tool, allowing doctors to interpret results alongside clinical judgment.

6

Report Generation

A structured diagnostic report with predictions, confidence scores, and heatmaps can be downloaded as a PDF for medical records.
